Welcome to RICH REWARDS TRADING 276 (PTY) LTD

RICH REWARDS TRADING 276 (PTY) LTD is a leading and dynamic enterprise committed to empowering communities and delivering world-class electrical and civil engineering contracting services. Our vision is to be a cornerstone in infrastructure development, fostering socio-economic growth and creating sustainable jobs in South Africa.

With a strong foundation built on professionalism, quality, and a commitment to safety, we strive to exceed stakeholder expectations by delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest engineering standards.

Executive Summary

RICH REWARDS TRADING 276 (PTY) LTD envisions a future as a primary contracting and supplying enterprise across Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Engineering, Mining, Logistics, Building, Construction, Security, Cleaning Services, Stationery, Educational materials and Pest Control. Our core mission is to empower black youth in South Africa, contributing to sustainable socio-economic growth and creating more sustainable jobs for communities. We are committed to developing and capacitating local government-funded community development projects and fostering vibrant community initiatives.

Vision and Mission Statement

Our Vision: To be counted among the best contracting service providers, utilizing local authorities, municipalities, and building services, thereby maintaining and enhancing our leadership role in increasing our penetration and market share. This implies:

  • Delighting stakeholders through uncompromised quality, on-time service delivery within budget, and high engineering standards.
  • Conducting business ethically and professionally at all times.
  • Attracting and retaining top technical management skills by creating a thriving work environment.
  • Adopting a safe and environmentally friendly working culture.
  • Building sustainable relationships with our stakeholders.
  • Ensuring sustainable growth and profitability.
  • Playing an active business and social role in assisting the government in meeting its millennium development goals.

Our Mission: A world-class construction service that meets the needs of our valued customers. We embody teamwork, commitment, efficiency, and excellence in all our endeavors.

Work With Us

Privacy Policy

At RICH REWARDS TRADING 276 (PTY) LTD, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details when you:

  • Fill out a contact form.
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or updates.
  • Apply for employment or partnership opportunities.

We also collect non-personal information, such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited, to improve our website's functionality and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Send periodic emails regarding company news or relevant updates (if you opt-in).
  • Process applications for jobs or partnerships.
  • Maintain internal records and comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored in secure networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems and are required to keep the information confidential.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your consent, except for trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.
