About Us

Executive Summary

RICH REWARDS TRADING 276 (PTY) LTD envisions a future as a primary contracting and supplying enterprise across Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Engineering, Mining, Logistics, Building, Construction, Security, Cleaning Services, Stationery, Educational materials and Pest Control. Our core mission is to empower black youth in South Africa, contributing to sustainable socio-economic growth and creating more sustainable jobs for communities. We are committed to developing and capacitating local government-funded community development projects and fostering vibrant community initiatives.

Vision and Mission Statement

Our Vision: To be counted among the best contracting service providers, utilizing local authorities, municipalities, and building services, thereby maintaining and enhancing our leadership role in increasing our penetration and market share. This implies:

  • Delighting stakeholders through uncompromised quality, on-time service delivery within budget, and high engineering standards.
  • Conducting business ethically and professionally at all times.
  • Attracting and retaining top technical management skills by creating a thriving work environment.
  • Adopting a safe and environmentally friendly working culture.
  • Building sustainable relationships with our stakeholders.
  • Ensuring sustainable growth and profitability.
  • Playing an active business and social role in assisting the government in meeting its millennium development goals.

Our Mission: A world-class construction service that meets the needs of our valued customers. We embody teamwork, commitment, efficiency, and excellence in all our endeavors.
